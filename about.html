Bio

I am an artist and graphics programmer based in Berlin, where I am lecturing part-time at Universität der Künste. I co-founded in 2006 the IOCOSE art collective.

I am freelancing as a graphics programmer and 3D generalist, past collaborations include artists, design studios, software companies and music labels such as Tomás Saraceno Studio, Heiner Franzen, Mechatronica, FIELD, Studio NAND, HERE, Studio ANF, Heinrich Dressel.
