Bio

Davide Prati’s video animations and still images depict a world of abstract shapes inspired by the forms and surfaces of trees and birds.

He uses computer programming, computer graphics software and sampling of digital archives as the foundation of his art practice.

Davide Prati was born in 1983 in Italy, and lives in Berlin. Since 2019 he is a member of the Uferhallen Verein, Berlin.
