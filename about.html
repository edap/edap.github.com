Bio

Davide Prati is a visual artist and interactive developer working across many digital disciplines to create real-time generative art, interactive websites and immersive experiences.

Since 2015, he is developing a collection of open-source software to generate programmatically shapes of flowers, leaves and trees.

Since 2006, he is part of the art collective IOCOSE, collective that has exhibited internationally in different venues, including Venice Biennale (2011, 2013), Science Gallery (Dublin, 2012) Jeu de Paume (Paris, 2011), Transmediale (Berlin, 2013, 2015)
