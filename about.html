Bio

Davide Prati was born in 1983 in Italy, and lives in Berlin, where he is lecturing (part-time) at Universität der Kunste. He co-founded in 2006 the IOCOSE art collective.

He works in computer programming and digital animation to explore, how software generated representations of light, shadow, movement and surface relate to our perception of reality.

Freelancing

I am a freelancing as a graphics programmer, interactive developer and 3D generalist.

Past collaborations include artists, design studios, software companies and music labels such as Tomás Saraceno Studio, Heiner Franzen, Mechatronica, FIELD, Studio NAND, HERE, Studio ANF, Heinrich Dressel.

I have professional experience programming realtime applications, websites and installations using threejs, p5.js, openFrameworks or Processing. I can create custom software for generating 3d models or textures. I am familiar with the process of making 3D video animations using Blender .
