Bio

Davide Prati’s video animations and still images focus on abstract shapes and surfaces inspired by organic forms.

He uses computer programming, computer graphics software and sampling of digital images as the foundation of his art practice.

Davide Prati was born in 1983 in Italy, and lives in Berlin, where he is lecturing (part-time) at Universität der Kunste. Since 2019 he is a member of the Uferhallen Verein, Berlin. He is one of the founding member of the IOCOSE art collective.
